About the Event

Are you ready for some Tuga fun?

In a far far away land, called Portugal, resides the barbaric kingdom of Porto where the mythical “TugaJam” has been rumored around... The natives, also known as Tugues, believe that during a foggy yet hot summer night, Don Sebastião, “The Wizard”, and his fellow champions will come forth to announce a new era. An era full of infinite fun, adventure, and putas e binho verde. They are said to have a strong faith in their cause. Travelling the lands, spreading the portuguese rowdy spirit, searching for the wildest souls across the mainland. They will claim the title of the BEST JAM from our mighty mighty Europe and are to gather between the 13 to 16 of July to celebrate this milestone in the JAMian history.
